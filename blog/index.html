Creative Process for Logo Design - CTE

Ethan Quaresma

October 11th, 2021

 In Advanced Media Arts, we were given the task of creating the next CTE logo. The Acronym CTE stands for Career Technical Education. It provides students with technical skills and the knowledge and training necessary to succeed in the career of their choice. Unfortunately, the original logo is quite bland and doesn’t really help unfamiliar eyes understand what the logo represents. I decided to take action by redesigning the logo with the previous issues in mind. Without further ado, I present my redesigned logo:



 One of the biggest things that stands out in this logo is the contrast between the background and the logo itself. I chose this background color to make the logo stand out much more than it would on white or black. The colors of the logo itself are forked from the original color scheme, as I felt like that was definitely an important detail to bring to the new logo. Secondly, the logo is in the shape of a gear, where the gear represents an important path to learning and a gearbox represents education as a whole. Lastly, the letters C, T, and E are aligned in a staircase fashion. This represents that CTE is a pathway that provides the students a skillset and training for their future.

 To conclude, I feel that my logo has a more significant meaning than the original. It includes details that the previous logo doesn’t, and aligns with the original idea of having an important piece to complete the whole.
